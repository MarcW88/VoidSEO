How We Prioritize

Our Decision Framework

Every feature request goes through our own VOID Loop process:

  • Vision: Does this solve a real SEO problem worth automating?
  • Objective: Can we define clear success metrics and user value?
  • Implementation: Do we have the resources and expertise to build it well?
  • Deep Dive: Will this advance the SEO engineering discipline?

Community Impact

How many users will benefit? How often will they use it? Does it solve a common pain point?

Technical Feasibility

Can we build it with our current resources? Does it align with our technical architecture?

Strategic Alignment

Does it advance our mission of transparent, observable SEO automation?
