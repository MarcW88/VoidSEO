Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with VoidSEO?

Start with our Getting Started guide to run your first workflow in 10 minutes. Then join our Discord community to connect with other builders.

Is VoidSEO really free?

Yes! We offer free preview access to our apps with example data. You can also access the complete VOID Loop framework and community for free. Paid tiers unlock functional apps with real data.

Can I contribute my own workflows?

Absolutely! We encourage sharing workflows built with the VOID Loop. Share them in our Discord #workflow-sharing channel or submit them to our GitHub repository.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

You can report bugs and request features through our GitHub Issues or in the Discord #bug-reports and #feature-requests channels.

Do you offer enterprise support?

Yes! For enterprise customers, we offer dedicated support, custom integrations, and on-site training. Contact us at enterprise@voidseo.dev for more information.

How can I stay updated on new features?

Follow our public roadmap, join our Discord community, or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates and feature announcements.
